About Clown Triggerfish

- Clown Triggerfish (*Balistoides conspicillum*) is a striking reef fish known for its bold patterns and vibrant colors.

- Native to the Indo-Pacific region, it has a different look with a combination of black, white, and yellow patterns.

- species is admired for its beauty and is a popular choice among marine aquarists.
